# outreach-cli

Track and optimize your community outreach efforts with data-driven insights.

## Problem

Independent developers often struggle with:
- Fear of sharing products publicly
- Spending time helping others without getting feedback or conversions
- Not knowing which platforms or post styles work best

## Solution

outreach-cli helps you track community posts with UTM links, measure click-through rates, and identify what actually converts.

## Features

- Track posts across multiple platforms (Reddit, Twitter, HackerNews, etc)
- Monitor clicks and conversions with UTM parameters
- Generate weekly reports showing best performing platforms
- Get data-driven sharing templates based on successful posts
- Simple SQLite database (no external services needed)

## Tips

1. Use UTM parameters in your links to track clicks accurately
2. Update metrics regularly to get meaningful insights
3. Focus on platforms with highest conversion rates
4. Analyze top posts to understand what resonates with your audience
